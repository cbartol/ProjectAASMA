Templates for Developpers

You can install templates for your Hoshimi Project.

First step is to download the template of your language:

 

Download Visual Basic Template

Download C# Template

 

Next, double click on it and install it.

Then, when you will launch Visual Studio 2005 or Visual C# Express 2005 or Visual Basic Express 2005 and you create a new project, you'll see in My Templates section Project Hoshimi Template.

I recommand to to to choose the  %Project Hoshimi Folder%/Developpers for the location of your new project. (otherwise, you'll have to update references and compilation output folder).
