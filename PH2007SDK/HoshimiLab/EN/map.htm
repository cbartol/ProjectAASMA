The tissue

You will be injected in a tissue. In this tissue, there is different areas:

Black area This is bone so you can't go there.
Red area Density of blood is normal in this area so your nanobots go at normal speed.
Blue area Density of blood is medium, so your nanobots go a little bit slower.
Green area High density of blood, so your nanobots go very slowly here.
Stream It's a stream of white cells. If your nanobots go in this stream, it goes faster if it goes in the same direction and slowler if it goes against.
Explorer is not influenced by the density of blood. On the other hand, streams affects it.
   
AZN Point This is where you can collect AZN points.
Hoshimi Point This is where you must build NanoNeedles.
Objective Point To accomplish NavigationObjectives and UniqueNavigationsObjectives, you must reach some points. Those points are located with this icon.
