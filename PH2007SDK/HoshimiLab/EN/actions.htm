Actions

Here is the list of possible actions in Project Hoshimi for Beginners :

MoveTo Your Nanobot go to the point.
Nanobots:
  • NanoAI,
  • Collector,
  • Container,
  • Protector,
  • Explorer.
CollectFrom Your Nanobot try to collect AZN molecules where it is.
Don't forget to set the number of turns you want to collect.
In each turn, you collect x molecule where x is the value of CollectTransferSpeed characteristic of your Nanobot.
Nanobots:
  • Collector,
  • Container.
TransferTo Your Nanobot try to transfer AZN molecules to a NanoNeedle where it is.
Don't forget to set the number of turns you want to transfer.
In each turn, you collect x molecule where x is the value of CollectTransferSpeed characteristic of your Nanobot.
Nanobots:
  • Collector,
  • Container.
Attack Your Nanobot attack at the location.
If the distance between this location and the location of your Nanobot is greater than its DefenseDistance characteristic, attack fails.
Don't forget to precise number of turn it attacks.
Nanobots:
  • Collector,
  • Protector,
Idle Your Nanobot will do nothing during the number of turn you enter.
Nanobots:
  • NanoAI,
  • Collector,
  • Container,
  • Protector,
  • Explorer.
BuildNeedle Your NanoAI build a NanoNeedle where it is located. BEWARE: if you have more than 40 Nanobots, it will refuse to build it.
Nanobots:
  • NanoAI.
BuildBlocker Your NanoAI build a NanoBlocker where it is located. BEWARE: if you have more than 40 Nanobots, it will refuse to build it.
Nanobots:
  • NanoAI.
ForceAutoDestruction Your Nanobot is destroyed immediatly.
Nanobots:
  • Collector,
  • Container,
  • Protector,
  • Explorer.
