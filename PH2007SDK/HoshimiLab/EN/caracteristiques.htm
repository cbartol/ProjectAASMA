Characteristics

Each nanobots have its own characteristics. In Project Hoshimi for Beginners, you can't change it. In Project Hoshimi for Developpers, you can modify it.

ContainerCapacity

This is the maximum amount of AZN molecules it can carry.
CollectTransfertSpeed It's the number of molecules it can collect from a AZN point or transfer into a NanoNeedle during a turn.
For example, if your nanobot have a CollectTransfertSpeed set to 5, in 4 turns, it will collect 20 molecules.
It can't carry more than its ContainerCapacity.
Scan It's the distance of vision of your Nanobot. It will detect all nanobots from Pierre team or from another team. This information is known by all nanobots of your team.
MaxDamage When your nanobot attack a Pierre's Nanobot, it is the maximum of damage it can inflinct per turn.
DefenseDistance Your nanobot can't attack a Pierre's Nanobot which is further than this distance.
Constitution It's the number of hit points of your nanobots at the beginning of its life. If your hit points reach 0, your nanobot explode.
