Test & Results

It's time to test your strategy.

In Project Hoshimi, there is two viewers provided. One is in 2D, the other in 3D. Go to Options to select your viewer.

To test your strategy, click on "Test Strategy button". The viewer is launched. Click on Start Game button and see your Nanobots injected in the tissue.

You can stop the game at any time but you can launch only one game at the same time.

A game is 5mn long (1500 turns).

Once you close the viewer, you stop the game and you'll see the results in the Results tab.

You'll see if you reached the objectives and when.

If you reached all objectives, a congratulation message must appears.

When you test your strategy, it is saved, but you can also click on the "Save Strategy button".

You can also Export your strategy. It will then appear in the Developper area. You can at this time run games against developpers ;-)
