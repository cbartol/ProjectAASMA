Lesson 4

Introduction:

You can meet a bad boy in the tissue: Pierre team. Pierre can build NeuroController (Nanobots that can't move but can fire against your Nanobots) and Protectors.

In this mission, you have only one objective: there must be no NeuroControllers in the tissue at turn 1400. So you will have to destroy all of them.

Pierre's team:

Select first an Injection Point and launch a game. You see that at the beginning of the game, Pierre have already 6 NeuroControllers on the tissue. Those NeuroControllers will always appear at the same locations.

For example, there is one at (133, 133).

Squads:

Create a Protector Squad and check the Attack Enemy option.

Actions:

Go back to Action tab and ask your Protector Sqaud to go to these location.

As it is near the NeuroController, you'll see that they are under attack but don't do anything, still trying to move to (133, 133) point. The bad news is that they are destroyed before reaching this point. Why it didn't attack the NeuroController? This is because it didn't finish its action (MoveTo).

Scan characteristic:

Each Nanobot have a Scan characteristic. When the distance between the Protector and the NeuroController is lower than this value, your Protector can see it.

DefenseDistance characteristic:

Each Nanobot have also a DefenseDistance characteristic. It can't fire a point which is greater than this distance.

Your goal:

So your goal, is to be near the NeuroController to see it and be able to attack it, but not to near that it can fire you.

Make some try and destroy all those bad NeuroControllers.
