Lesson 3

Introduction:

In this lesson, you will learn how to create NanoNeedle, collect AZN molecules and transfer those molecules in your NanoNeedle.

NanoNeedles:

To build a NanoNeedle, you use the BuildNeedle action of your NanoAI. The NanoNeedle will be built where your NanoAI is.

So to build a NanoNeedle on a Hoshimi Point, you'll have first to Move your NanoAI to the Hoshimi Point and then build the NanoNeedle.

(It is important to gain points to build your NanoNedle on Hoshimi Points).

Injection Point:

So first, choose an Injection Point near a Hoshimi Point. For example , choose here (150, 50).

Build the NanoNeedle:

Add two actions to your NanoAI:

  • MoveTo (143 54) where is the Hoshimi Point,
  • BuildNeedle.

Test your strategy and you will see your first NanoNeedle.

Collecting AZN Molecules:

Collectors (and Containers) allow you to extract AZN Molecules from AZN Points.

A collector can stock 20 molecules and can extract (and transfer) 5 molecules/turn.

A container can stock 50 molecules and can extract (and transfer) 5 molecules/turn.

So to collect AZN, containers seems better, but a container can't defend itself against enemy.

Create a new squad of collector with 1 collector only.

Go back to the Action tab and add it the following actions:

  • MoveTo AZN point at (154, 54)
  • Collect AZN molecules at (154, 54) with the option "Number of turn = 4".

So your NanoCollector will be injected at the Injection Point, will move to the AZN point and then collect 4 turns*5 = 20 molecules.

At the same time, your NanoAI will build the NanoNeedle at the Hoshimi Point.

Transfer the molecules to the NanoNeedle:

To gain points, you must transfer the molecules in the NanoNeedle. You will ask your NanoCollector to do so. Add to it the following actions:

  • MoveTo the NanoNeedle (143, 54),
  • TransferTo at (143, 54) during 4 turns.

Test your strategy: you'll see your NanoCollector collecting and transfering AZN molecules to the NanoNeedle. It will go back 5 times to the AZN Point because the NanoNeedle can stock up to 100 molecules. So it take quite a long time to fill the NanoNeedle.

You can see too that your NanoCollector will not move anymore when the NanoNeedle will be filled, because it is trying to trasfer its molecules but can't do that.

To go faster, increase the number of Collectors in your squad in the Squad tab. You can fill the NanoNeedle in one time only.

Score objective

There is only one objective in this mission, it is a score objective: you must reach 700 points before turn 700. So you'll have to create more NanoNeedles, and more squads.

Then, try to reach the highest score the quickless you can.
